Sourdough bread

This bread is thought to have originated in Egypt in 1500 BC and is made using a long fermentation process with natural yeasts and lactobacilli. The fermentation process creates lactic acid, which gives the bread its signature sour flavor.

Last updated 3 mins ago
Brioche

This French bread is made from enriched dough that includes milk and eggs, which gives it a light and fluffy texture. The outside of brioche should be shiny and golden, while the inside should be soft and spongy. The eggs also give brioche a more yellow appearance than other breads.
